~The inverse of a conditional statement is ~p -> ~q, or "If not p then not q."
+The converse of a conditional statement is q -> p, or "If q then p."
-The contrapositive of a conditional statement is ~q -> ~p, or "If not q then not p."
/The biconditional of a conditional statement is p <-> q, or "p if and only if q."
